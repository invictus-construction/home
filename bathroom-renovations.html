Bathroom Renovations — from concept to completion

We design and build bathroom renovations that balance functionality with elegant finishes. Our team coordinates plumbing, electrical, tiling, cabinetry, and fixtures so your bathroom looks and performs exactly as planned.

Quality & materials

We partner with trusted suppliers for tile, fixtures and cabinetry. We use industry-standard waterproofing, moisture-resistant backer boards and precisely-installed fixtures to ensure longevity and safety in wet areas.

Renovation steps

  1. Design consultation and layout planning — we create options to suit your needs and budget.
  2. Detailed estimate and material selection — transparent costs and scheduling.
  3. Demolition & rough-in — framing, plumbing and electrical updates are completed to code.
  4. Install finishes — tile, cabinetry, fixtures and trim.
  5. Final inspection, client walkthrough and maintenance guidance.

Design flexibility

Choose fixtures, tile sizes, cabinet styles and lighting. We provide recommendations and mock-ups; final choices are flexible during design so the finished bathroom reflects your tastes.
