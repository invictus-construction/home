Our Services — Roofing, Tiling & Home Renovations in Sarnia

Garage Construction

Commercial-quality builds, made locally. We deliver full garage & car-port construction from the concrete slab up — framing, insulation, siding, roofing and finishing. We use structural-grade materials and proven techniques so your new garage lasts and looks great.

How we plan & build (step-by-step)
  1. Consultation & site survey — we verify dimensions, drainage and local codes.
  2. Design options & client choices — you pick layout, door type, siding and trim.
  3. Permits & scheduling — we prepare drawings, pull permits and schedule crews.
  4. Foundation & framing — concrete slab and robust framing installed by experienced crews.
  5. Weatherproofing & finishes — siding, roofing, insulation, and quality trims.
  6. Final inspection & handover — we test, clean, and walk you through maintenance tips.

Client flexibility: Choose finishes, doors, paint, insulation level and internal layout — we’ll provide hands-on guidance and material samples so your garage fits your budget and style.

New Roofs

Durable roofing & weather protection. From inspections and repairs to full roof replacements, we use market-leading shingles, underlayment and flashing to protect your home. Our workmanship and quality materials reduce leaks and extend roof life.

Our roofing process
  1. Thorough roof inspection, photodocumentation & estimate.
  2. Material selection and budget options (shingle quality, metal roofing, vents).
  3. Preparation & tear-off (if needed) with proper disposal.
  4. Underlayment, flashing and ventilation installed to code.
  5. Final walk-through, cleanup, and warranty documentation.

Quality & warranty: We install high-performance underlayment and premium shingles; we follow best practices for ventilation and flashing to protect against ice, rain and wind.

Design choices: Choose shingle color, profile, trims and gutter options — we’ll present samples and recommendations tailored to your home.

Tiling Floors

Precision tiling & durable finishes. We install tile for floors, showers, backsplashes and wet areas using proven substrate preparation and water-proofing systems. Our tight grout lines and careful layout create durable, attractive results.

Tile installation process
  1. Surface assessment & substrate preparation — we remove old finishes if necessary and level floors.
  2. Waterproofing & growth control — use of membranes, decoupling and correct backer boards for longevity.
  3. Layout and pattern planning — tile orientation, grout lines and focal features are reviewed with you in advance.
  4. Precision setting, grouting and sealing — we use premium adhesives and grout with color-matched sealers.
  5. Clean, inspect and handover with care instructions.

Choice & style: Pick your tiles, grout color, trim and special features. We'll provide layout mockups so you know exactly how the finished space looks before installation.

Bathroom Renovations

Bathrooms built with durability and style. Whether it's a simple refresh or a full gut-and-replace, we manage plumbing, tiling, cabinetry, fixtures and finishing to deliver a functional and beautiful result.

Bathroom renovation workflow
  1. Project scope & design — discuss goals, layout options and preferred fixtures.
  2. Detailed estimate & timeline — transparent pricing and milestone schedule.
  3. Demolition & rough-in — plumbing, electrical and framing done to code.
  4. Tile, waterproofing, cabinetry and fixtures installed precisely.
  5. Finishes, caulking, final plumbing hookups and client walkthrough.

Client-driven choices: From tiles to faucets, you control the finishes — we guide material selection and ensure the design fits your budget and lifestyle.
