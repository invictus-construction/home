Garage & Car Port Construction — What to expect

Invictus Construction builds durable, code-compliant garages and car ports for Sarnia homeowners. We manage the entire process — foundation and drainage, structural framing, roofing, siding, insulation, and final finishes — using structural-grade lumber, premium fasteners, and roofing materials rated for our local climate.

Quality materials & workmanship

We use industry trusted materials: concrete mixes suited for slab loads and drainage, engineered lumber and metal connectors, weather-rated siding and high-quality roofing products. Every step is inspected before moving on to the next phase so workmanship is consistent and durable.

Step-by-step process

  1. Initial consultation & site assessment — measurements, drainage plan and design options.
  2. Design & approval — choose layout, overhead doors, windows, and finishes.
  3. Permits & scheduling — we obtain required permits and plan the build phases.
  4. Foundation & concrete work — form, pour and cure to ensure long-term strength.
  5. Framing & sheathing — structural framing installed by experienced crews.
  6. Roofing & exterior finishes — weatherproofing, siding, and roofing completed to code.
  7. Interior finishing & handover — wiring, doors, hardware and final inspection.

Client flexibility & choices

You decide the layout, door systems, insulation level and finishes. We provide material samples and recommendations — from garage door styles to interior flooring and shelving options — and update the timeline and budget as choices are finalized.
