Professional Tiling & Flooring

Precision tiling that combines durability with aesthetic quality. We handle wet areas, floors, showers, backsplashes and transitions — prepared with the right substrates and waterproofing so your tile performs for years.

Why our tile work stands out

  • Correct subfloor & substrate prep (decoupling and leveling).
  • Waterproof membranes and correct drainage for showers and wet areas.
  • Clean grout lines and high-quality grout sealing for long life.
  • Professional layout planning so tile patterns look intentional and balanced.

Step-by-step tile process

  1. Site assessment and tile selection guidance.
  2. Preparation: remove existing flooring or prepare substrate.
  3. Waterproofing and layout/distribution checks.
  4. Precise tile setting, grouting, sealing and cleanup.

Design choices

Clients choose tile size, color, layout and grout finish; we provide mockups and sample boards so you can visualize the end result before setting begins.
